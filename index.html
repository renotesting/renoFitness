About Reno

I am AFLCA Certified Resistance Training Fitness Trainer. Certified Fitness Kickboxing Coach. I am Helping local Calgarian living in healthy and fitness life style since 2016.

I am using weight training and fitness kickboxing training to help my clients consistently improve themselves in the long term general fitness journey. 

Whether your goal is losing weight, gaining muscle, eating healthier, or even all — I make sure you don’t stress the details and I have customized solutions that fit your needs.
