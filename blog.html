Why Weight / Resistance Training?

Weight Training, Resistance Training, Muscle Training etc, they all are the same thing.

What are some resistance training tips from RenoFitness?

1) Resistance training increases muscle strength by making your muscles work against a weight or force.
2) Different forms of resistance training include free weights, weight machines, resistance bands and your own body weight.
3) A beginner needs to train two or three times per week to gain the maximum benefit.
4) Complete the Par-Q form and consult with professionals, such as your doctor, exercise physiologist, physiotherapist or registered exercise professional, before you start a new fitness program.
5) Rest each muscle group for at least 48 hours to maximise gains in strength and size.
6) Varying your workouts can help you push past a training plateau.

A note from Reno:

I recommend Resistance Training to the following people:

1) If you are a beginner, want to know and learn more effective fitness training
2) If you want to gain more muscle mass
3) If you want to manage your weight
4) If you want to learn more about nutrition intake and diet
